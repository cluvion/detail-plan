About Dr. Orn

A Pioneer. A Visionary.

Dr. Onuma Phanapiwat — known globally as Dr. Orn — is one of Asia's most distinguished hair restoration surgeons. A graduate of the prestigious Chulalongkorn University School of Medicine, she has dedicated over 14 years to perfecting the art and science of permanent hair restoration. She is the inventor of the DOHT (Direct Optimized Hair Transplantation) method and was the first surgeon in Thailand to pioneer the Long Hair FUE technique, allowing patients to assess natural-looking density and hairline design before even leaving the operating theatre.

Every single procedure is personally performed by Dr. Orn — never delegated, never compromised. This philosophy of absolute surgical accountability, combined with her dual-accreditation from both the American Board of Hair Restoration Surgery (ABHRS) and the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery (ISHRS), places her in the top tier of hair restoration specialists globally.

Link to full YouTube case study video. This email speaks directly to the prospect's own identity — they see themselves in the success stories. Subject: "What a Sydney banker said 6 months after his DOHT procedure."

04
Objection Handling: Safety, Costs, Travel & Recovery

Day 6. Directly and confidently addresses the 5 biggest objections: (1) "Is it really safe in Thailand?" — ABHRS/ISHRS accreditation, laminar-flow OR, BDMS hospital partnership. (2) "What if something goes wrong?" — 24/7 post-op line, 12-month follow-up protocol. (3) "How do I manage travel?" — dedicated coordinator, hotel partnerships, visa-free entry for AU/SG. (4) "What about my job?" — minimal downtime, scab-concealment protocol, back to office in 5 days. (5) "Is the quality lower?" — FUE Europe membership, 5-star international reviews. Subject: "5 reasons patients worry — and why they fly anyway."

05
Scarcity: "Only 8 International Slots Per Month"

Day 10. Dr. Orn personally performs every procedure — which means capacity is intentionally limited. This email communicates scarcity with authenticity. Current month's availability is shown. A countdown to the next available slot is displayed. Includes a WhatsApp direct link for instant booking enquiry. Subject: "March is almost full — 3 international slots remaining."

06
Book Your Consultation: Free 30-Min Video Call with Dr. Orn

Day 14. Final CTA: Book a complimentary 30-minute private video consultation with Dr. Orn. Calendly link with AU + SG time zone options. Positioned as: "This is not a sales call — it's a clinical consultation." Re-emphasises: no obligation, no cost, no pressure. Clinches the relationship between prospect and Dr. Orn personally. Subject: "Your free 30-minute consultation with Dr. Orn — choose your time."
